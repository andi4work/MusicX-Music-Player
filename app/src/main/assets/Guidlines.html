• FloatingWidget : 
    Hold to move anywhere.

    Long press to maximize.

    Tap anywhere on screen to minimize.

    Hold and move below center of screen to remove FloatingWidget.
  • PlayingView1 : 
    Swipe up and down to play prev/next.

    Slide right/left from playingView to view available lyrics/albumCover.

    Slide right to view queuelist.

    Drag,Drop and remove(from right) in queueList.
  • PlayingView2 : 
    Slide right/left from playingView to view available lyrics/albumCover.
  • PlayingView3 : 
    Slide up/down from albumcover to view/hide available lyrics.

    Drag,Drop and remove(from right) in queueList.
  • PlayingView 4 & 5 : 
    Slide right/left from playingView to view available lyrics/albumCover.

    Click on queueButton to see queueList

    Drag,Drop and remove (from right) in queueList.
  • PlayingView 6 : 
    Slide from bottom to top for queueList.

    Drag,Drop and remove (from right) in queueList.

    Single click on control to view available lyrics.

    Double click on control to go back home.

    Single click lyrics to view coverView.
  • Equalizer : 
    Slide right/left from bottom to see other effects.
  • Lyrics :
